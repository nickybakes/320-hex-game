Break the magical hex gems to create a powerful, combo breaking spell!

Quick Guide

  • Use Q and E to rotate hexes counterclockwise and clockwise, respectively.
  • Click and drag to draw a line between hexes.
  • Connect segments of matching colors to break the hexes and score!
  • Press Escape to pause
  • Press I to toggle icons on and off
  • Press M to mute/unmute sound effects
